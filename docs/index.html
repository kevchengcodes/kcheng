ABOUT ME


Welcome to my page. I'm Kevin, and I am a software engineer at Swoop Search. I'm currently working remotely in Seattle and having a great time building position, navigation, and timing solutions into our edge computing platforms.

I joined Swoop Search in November 2020 after almost four years at Boeing. During my time there, I created predictive algorithms in Python/SQL, real-time simulations in MATLAB/Simulink, and flight guidance optimization software in C/C++.

I started at Boeing in early 2017 after graduating with my BS and MS in Mechanical Engineering from Northwestern University (Go 'Cats). This is where my interest in software engineering began, and I am proud and grateful that I've been able to pursue my passion and build myself into a software generalist throughout my entire career.

Moving forward, I'm excited to continue innovating at the cutting-edge of technology and software. I am also studying computer science as part of the OMSCS program at Georgia Tech, aiming to graduate in 2022 with a specialization in Machine Learning.

Thank you for visiting my page. Please feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn, check out some of my projects I've been working on, or read through some of my blog posts.

-Kevin Cheng
